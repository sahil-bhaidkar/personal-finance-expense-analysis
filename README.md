# 📊 Financial Analytics Dashboard for Personal Wealth Management
- A data-driven dashboard to analyze income, expenses, savings, and investments for smarter financial decision-making.

## 🏅 Dashboard Structure

The dashboard is divided into multiple interactive pages for comprehensive financial analysis:

### 🏠 Overview
- High-level summary of financial health
- Key KPIs:
  - Total Income
  - Total Expenses
  - Net Savings
  - Savings Rate (%)
- Monthly financial trend visualization

---

### 💸 Daily Expenses
- Day-wise expense tracking
- Category-level spending insights
- Identification of high-spending patterns
- Helps in controlling daily budget leakage

---

### 📈 SIP Investments
- Tracks Systematic Investment Plans (SIP)
- Investment growth over time
- Contribution vs returns analysis
- Long-term wealth tracking

---

### 🏦 Debts
- Overview of all liabilities
- Outstanding balances tracking
- Repayment progress monitoring
- Helps in debt management and planning

---

### 🎯 Wishlist
- Tracks future financial goals and planned purchases
- Goal-based saving approach
- Helps prioritize spending decisions

---

### 🔔 Notifications
- Smart alerts and reminders
- Highlights unusual spending patterns
- Budget exceed alerts
- Financial discipline tracking
